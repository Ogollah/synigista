Services

Cloud Readiness Assessment

We understand that cloud readiness assessment is the first step to the cloud transformation journey that focuses on providing best counsel and options for the enterprise based on business their requirements and objectives. Assessment involves understanding the business drivers, goals, readiness state, compliance, optimization needs, and the impact of change management. As part of these services, we analyze work load, quality of service and security requirements, application usability and support, and technical platform details.

Database Management

Our Database Management services help ensure high performance and availability of business critical data assets. These offerings include design, implementation, migration, maintenance, consolidation and optimization of organizational database. Our proven expertise and a well-defined approach combined with our solution accelerators, toolkits and frameworks drive cost-effective, secure, and reliable services.

Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery

Our business continuity and disaster recovery (BCP/DR) services help clients ensure business continuity and adhere to policies and domain specific regulatory measures. Some of the main areas of focus are risk assessment, strategy, architecture design, process implementation, testing and maintenance. Robust frameworks, templates, and toolkits enable organizations to assess, design, and maintain BCP/DR successfully. Consultants with diverse industry experience and technology expertise help businesses meet response time objective (RTO) and recovery point objective (RPO) efficiently. Automated processes, predictive analytics and intuitive dashboards drive data driven management, and enhance operational efficiency and system availability.

Consulting

Synygista Group Ltd provides next generation training and consulting solution both to individuals and to corporate. State-of-the art management and technical training is customized for corporate clients and individual participants. We deliver solutions to corporate clients and individuals designed to meet the complex needs of today's business environment with an accurate understanding of their unique business requirements. Our dedicated Trainer/Consultants have extensive, successful corporate experience in successfully delivering effective business solutions. Other associates include a variety of technical specialists with experience in software services who provide practical, proven, and customized solutions.

Cloud Transformation Services

Synygista Group Ltd offers Cloud Transformation services that help analyze the existing setup, and design a consolidated and optimized roadmap to align transformation initiatives with business objectives. Our well-defined and structured approach helps identify the right cloud platform to enhance productivity, improve flexibility and security, and achieve significant cost savings.

Migration Services

Synygista Group Ltd helps clients to seamlessly migrate to cloud while ensuring connectivity, security, and flexibility as well as integration of existing applications. We ensure that there’s no single point of failure, we size instance as necessary, we plan for growth of your applications, we secure your data in transit and at rest and we provide adequate level of redundancy. These services enable a successful move to efficient data center environment, reduce costs and downtime, and enhance productivity.
